aphorism.io

aph·o·rism

/ˈafəˌrizəm/

a pithy observation that contains a general truth, such as, “Parents are the last people on earth who ought to have children.”

How To Play

  1. 1. Create a game (or join one) and link it to your friends
  2. 2. You have 120 seconds to write an aphorism about the word at top of the screen
  3. (For example: Ludum Dare -> "Ludum Dare is a lot like life, if you sleep you're wasting your time")
  4. 3. Vote on your favorite/the most "deep" aphorism
  5. 4. Repeat the process for 5 rounds of thought provoking entertainment!
